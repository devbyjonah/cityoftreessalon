Scheduling an Appointment

City of Trees Salon always accepts walkins; however, many days we are booked throughout the day beforehand and walk ins will experience long wait times. If you know you will be coming in, please schedule an appoinment so that we don't keep you waiting!

At this time we are only setting appoinments over the phone and by email. Please send us a message with the contact form below and we will get back to you with available times for the next week.
